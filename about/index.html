About

Posted on Feb 17, 2024

Hi, how are you? I hope this homepage will share my thoughts on physics and perhaps enlighten you. Also, I want to record my journey into the realms of physics. This homepage may work as my portfolio.

I’m interested in simulating physical phenomenons. Not everyone can simulate or let alone draw physical equations in their mind. To help people persue for their physics degree, I’m planning to share simulation of textbook level problems.

I’m currently a research student at SQS lab under prof Jeon Kun Rok. In the lab, I am studying Josephson junction, Josephson diode and Gilbert damping.

I would like to thank Dong Chan Kim, Jun Ho Kang, Jun Sik Yu for giving me motivations and helping hands to write this homepage.
